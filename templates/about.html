About Our Project

Welcome to our advanced healthcare management system designed to enhance patient care and streamline medical processes. Our system integrates a sophisticated deep learning image detection model that identifies and diagnoses common foot diseases, such as athlete's foot and foot corn, from uploaded images. Patients can easily upload their foot images through our user-friendly web interface, where our robust model provides accurate diagnoses and automated treatment recommendations. Additionally, our platform features a doctor channeling system, allowing patients to connect with healthcare professionals directly. After booking an appointment, patients receive a unique token via email for secure access and verification. This ensures a smooth and personalized healthcare experience, making it easier for patients to receive timely and appropriate care. Our system aims to improve healthcare accessibility and efficiency while ensuring that each patient receives the best possible service. We are committed to providing a seamless and secure experience for both patients and healthcare providers
